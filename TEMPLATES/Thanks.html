Ken Jee

This is the best channel you could find for Data science and career advice in my opinion. Ken was kind enough to reply to all my comments and has been an instrumental source of motivation for me.

Joshua Starmer

Joshua is an Assistant Professor at the University of North Carolina (UNC). At StatQuest, he is literally spoon-feeding us complicated Data science concepts. He is also very modest and fun to talk to !

Tina Huang

We study together three times a week and chat with her on her channel. Not only is she a great and fun source of information and motivation, she has also helped me stay accountable and true to the process.

Ritvik Kharkar

Ritvik is the Joshua Starmer of Mathematics. He has amazing videos where he explains advanced concepts and models with simple analogies.
